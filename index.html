The Solid Core Story

Building the Future of Kerala

Solid Core Bricks were born out of a necessity to provide a reliable, premium construction material that can face the relentless monsoons and coastal humidity of Kerala without compromising on structural integrity.

Unlike traditional clay or hollow bricks, our product is the result of rigorous research at NIT. We have optimized the composition to offer unparalleled load-bearing capacity, minimal water absorption, and an aesthetic industrial finish.

Product Composition & Strength

High Compressive Strength

Engineered for multi-story load-bearing walls without the need for excessive concrete pillars.

Low Water Absorption

Critical for Kerala's monsoons. Prevents efflorescence (white patches) and fungal growth.

Thermal Insulation

Keeps interiors cool during hot summers by reducing heat transfer through the walls.

Precision Molded

Perfect edges and uniform sizes reduce mortar consumption and plastering costs.
